The World's Most Powerful Rocket

The Falcon Heavy is the most powerful operational rocket in the world by a factor of two:

With the ability to lift into orbit nearly 64 metric tons (141,000 lb) The Falcon Heavy can lift more than twice the payload of the next closest operational vehicle - the Delta IV Heavy, at one-third the cost.
